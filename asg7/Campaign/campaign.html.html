Catherine the Great

The Empress of Russia that ruled in the 1700s that pushed the empire into greatness. She was one of the first nobles to open a house for abandoned children, created bureaucratic laws that included serfs, and established schools that taught different castes of society skilled labor. She spared no expense to bring world-reknowned geniuses at the time to Russia, and invested in many different intellectual organizations in many different countries.
