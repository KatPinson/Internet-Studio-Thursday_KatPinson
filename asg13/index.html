Dream Jobs:

Truth be told, my dream career would be a part-time writer/artist. Specifically, where I am able to alternate between the two past-times that I love: making fiber art sculptures and writing fiction. But in the short-term, I need to pay my bills. So while I stockpile some money, I picked a few safe jobs until I make it big doing what I love.


Underwriter

National Life Group Underwriter

Although people are reluctant to label our current economic status as a depression, statistics indicate it most likely is. The most secure jobs are the jobs that train you because they treat you like an investment and are more likely to keep you around after you are trained. National Life Group is an excellent choice in this regard because they keep a good portion of the employees they hire, but they also help you learn how to underwrite life insurance policies. They also have a lot of very good employee benefits.


Floral Designer Assistant

Floral Designer Assistant

I am pretty experienced with tending to plants and nature in general. My grandmother has the largest garden I've seen and takes excellent care of her plants since before I was born. She gave me her green thumb and I understand a lot about them.
