DnD's Nine Hells

Come take a trip to the nine hells! I insist.

Nine layers of Torment to Explore!

The nine hells is the afterlife awaiting lawful evil people in Faerun after their deaths. Nine hells—Avernus, Dis, Minauros, Phlegethos, Stygia, Malbolge, Maladomini, Cania, and Nessus—each unique in their awfulness. The entire realm is enclosed in Asmodeus' iron fist. But don't worry! You can get away with anything from torture to murder as long as you have the right paperwork!


You can sign contracts and make deals with all sorts of fiendish friends! Sell your soul! Unlimited Power! No regrets!

Enjoy these many fun realms and all of the entertainment they offer!
